Who Are We?

Dinamo Anatolia football club was founded by 6 Turkish friends in 2020 and is the only Turkish team that actively operates in the Canadian city of Vancouver.

Where We Are Playing?

Dinamo Anatolia club participated in the Knight Summer Soccer League organization in its first year with the support of its sponsors WESTLINE IMMIGRATION and SCN EDUCATION. Dinamo Anatolia missed its playoff chance in the last match in its first year.


Dinamo Anatolia club was accepted by Burnaby Men's Winter Soccer League in the 2020-2021 season. WESTLINE IMMIGRATION and EGITIMAL EDUCATION SERVICES have undertaken the jersey sponsorship of the 2020-2021 season.

Anatolia Etmology
The English-language name Anatolia derives from the Greek Ἀνατολή (Anatolḗ) meaning "the East" and designating (from a Greek point of view) eastern regions in general.
